Giving

Partner With Heaven

Giving is intended to be an act of worship, it is the believer’s opportunity to respond to the extravagant grace and glory of God. God generously meets the needs of His people, both physically and spiritually. Giving is an opportunity for His people to use those gifts to return honour and glory to Him. Just as He was extravagant in His giving, so too Christ’s followers have the opportunity to be extravagant in their giving back to Him.
